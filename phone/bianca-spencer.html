After some back and forth Spanish banter in late 2016, phone numbers were exchanged. A few months passed by, and Spencer & Bianca crossed paths again and planned to see each other at a local restaurant in Boca Raton. Both of them were accompanied by their friends that evening but they did not stop talking among themselves.

The energy kept on flowing, a lot of laughs, spanglish stories, sharing of traveling experiences that they both enjoyed, and there was an immediate spark that set off this fire of love.

There was a special connection between them, and their relationship kept on expanding and their whirlwind romance began. Spencer & Bianca share so many common interests like travel, culture, and athletics; They began to do more things together. As time passed by, their relationship expanded in every sense. They moved in together and welcomed a little puppy in their lives. This made their relationship even stronger.

The two of them traveled the world and began experiencing different sports such as skiing, hiking, biking, yoga, tennis, and horseback riding. Tennis has a very special part in their lives. Their first date was on the tennis courts, not long later Spencer proposed on the tennis courts. Bianca said yes, and now they are so excited for their next chapter together and they are ecstatic to be able to celebrate their love with their friends and family.
